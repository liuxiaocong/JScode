The types of information we collect:

1. User information you provide to us at account registration

When you create an account at Hashnest, you provide us with personal information that includes your contact information (email, name, and a password). You can also choose to add an active mobile number for SMS or Google Authenticator to be used for 2FA for additional account security.

2. Formal information you provide to us when authenticating user identity

When you request for account recovery or assistance and/or compliance purposes such as KYC, we require you to undergo user identity authentication. 3.1 Usage and log information

We use a variety of technologies, including cookies, local storage such as browser web storage or application data caches, databases, and server logs to monitor and collect log information sent by your browser. This log information may include: IP address, browser type, browser version, page of services you visit, access time and date, time spent on these pages, and more statistical data. In addition, we may use third-party services to collect, monitor, and analyse such information to enhance the functionality of our Services. These third-party service providers can only view the use of your personal data when needed to perform tasks on our behalf.

3.2 Transaction information

We may request for transaction information including e-wallet addresses, deposit and withdrawal snapshots, account balances, trade history, order status and payout data.

1. To provide and maintain our Services

Our primary purpose in collecting your personal information is to provide you with and maintain a secure, smooth, efficient, and customised Services.

We continuously measure and analyse your personal information to better understand the way you use and interact with our Services. We process your personal information when you contact us to resolve any questions, issues, or to troubleshoot problems when you use our Services. We may process your information in response to another customer’s request, as relevant. We cannot provide you with our Services without such information.

5. To enforce our terms in Terms of Use, Terms of Purchase and other agreements

We handle sensitive information, such as your identification and financial data, so it is very important for us and our customers that we continually review, investigate and prevent any potentially prohibited or illegal activities that violate our Terms of Use.

6. To ensure network and information security

We process your personal information in order to protect our Services and you. We use your usage data, online identifiers and log information to enhance security, monitor and verify identity or service access, combat spam, phishing or other malware or security risks. 7. To comply to legal and regulatory requirements

Some of our Services are subject to laws and regulations requiring us to collect and use your personal information, formal identification information, online identifiers, and/or usage data in certain ways.
